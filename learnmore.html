A Brief History

The Internet from 1950-1990

  • 1950: JCR Licklighter first envisioned the idea of the internet.

  • 1958: Creation of ARPA in response to Soviet launch of SPUTNIK.

  • 1961: Leonard Kleinrock creates packet switching.

  • 1962: The idea of the Hot Potato Rally was developed and BNN went to build the first imps.

  • 1965: Lawrence Roberts and Thomas Marill creates first wide area network.

  • 1969: ARPANET was launched.

  • 1971:E-mail was created.

  • 1972: The first international conference of computer communications.

  • 1977: Lawrence Landweber created the computer science network.

  • 1982: First public WAN initiated.

  • 1983: ARPANET transitions into TCP/IP.

  • 1989: Brewster Kahle invents first internet publishing system.

The Internet from 1990-2016

  • 1990:Internet Security Systems begin to be developed by Stephen Kent.

  • 1990: Linux is created by Linus Torvalds.

  • 1991: Email encryption is developed by Paul Zimmerman.

  • 1991: The World Wide Web is opened to the public.

  • 1995: The audio format, MP3, is created.

  • 1998: Google is establisheded by Larry Page and Sergen.

  • 1999: Craigslist is launched by Craig Newmark.
