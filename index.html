The History of the tool we use most

Some Basic History

Vint Cerf and Bob Kahn created the Internet. It was the result of an experiment by the Advanced Research Projects Agency, known as ARPA that was called ARPAnet. This was a defence department project. The Internet is made up of large, independently operated networks. There is no central control.
